### Reddit Post - r/InternetIsBeautiful

**Title**: I made a website that plays sitcom laugh tracks when you pause talking

**Body**:
I built this dumb little thing and it's been cracking me up.

autolaughtrack.com

It uses your microphone (browser only, nothing recorded) to detect when you stop talking. When you pause, it plays random sitcom sound effects - laugh tracks, applause, "awww" sounds, etc.

The longer your pause, the bigger the laugh.

Use cases I've discovered:
- Zoom meetings (everyone hated it, 10/10)
- Practicing presentations
- Just talking to yourself at home
- Telling stories at parties

It's free, no signup, works on mobile too. Made it with vanilla JS and Web Audio API.
